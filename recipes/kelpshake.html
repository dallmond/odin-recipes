To Die For Kelp Shake

Let's Make a Kelp Shake!

Ingredients:

  • Soy milk
  • Spinach
  • Kelp extract
  • Pineapple
  • Green apple
  • Banana

Steps:

  1. Blend all Ingredients together in a blender
  2. Spin around three times
  3. Serve in special kelp shake bottle (the final and most important step)
