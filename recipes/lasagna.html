Garfield's Lasagna Recipe

Let's Make Garfield's Lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Noodles
  • Mozzarella slices
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps:

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles)
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce
  5. Repeat the same layers two more times
  6. Cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes
  7. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes
  8. Let rest for 15 minutes prior to serving
