SpongeBob's Krabby Patty Recipe

Let's Make Spongebob's Krabby Patty!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef patties
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Cook the patty by grilling over medium heat for about 4 minutes each side
  2. Add cheese just before taking the patty off the grill
  3. Assemble the patty starting with the bottom bun - add the patty, then the lettuce, tomato, cheese, onion, and pickles
  4. Add ketchup and mustard to the bun, and top with the top bun
